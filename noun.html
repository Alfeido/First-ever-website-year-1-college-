The Sacred Grounds

This modestly-sized continent stretches across several climate regions, from hot in the North, through to cold in the South. The Sacred Grounds sits close to the tectonic boundaries; there are a few mountains; and large hills scattered. This continent has no volcanoes, and earthquakes recently became common. The winds in this region are generally calm, with large storms and extreme weather being common. The seas surrounding The Sacred Grounds are often tretcherous. Home to a large variety of creatures and plants, and a massive amount of those are found only in this region due to the falling star.

Shadow Death

Shadow Death is a sickness, it's name given by Dr H.Lucille, this is because it mimics death, seeking out everyone, all data regarding how one becomes infected is irradical, anomoalous. In fact it's very existence drove the Doctor to madness, he isolates himself in an observatory south of the Sacred Grounds.

Hand Bearers Of The Damned

Lanway is a Draegloth, an evil fiend/fey abomination, his race was rare but he himself was rarer, he was born with a blood condition, at first a curse leaving him the runt of his litter. He was left to die to the serpents of Cynan when Dr H Lucille stepped in. He ran experiments on Lanway in an attempt to unlock a new power that could cure Shadow Death. All tests ended and after 97 logged tests with over one-hundred not logged, Lanway was thrown out again, but changed, he could materialise his blood into one weapon , a bell which he could ring at certain frequencies to afflict various effects on his opponents. He wandered the Sacred Grounds until he encountered a force that granted him the title Lanway The Bellborne, Hand Knight.
